Memories Lounge Menu

The lounge menu using corporate colors and fonts has been created with an emphasis on the bar assortment and its description: all drinks are arranged in a strict hierarchy of categories and flavors.
The cocktail list is designed to convey the most important concept - all classic drinks are prepared exclusively according to IBA recipes, and the author's drinks are the quintessence of the knowledge and skills of the bar staff.

Bar Magazine

Based on the principle of glossy magazines with issues dedicated to the changing seasons, this material is a description of the inner workings of the bar with a description of the most important processes and interpretation of the concept: "why the bar uses hypoallergenic protein", "how we prepare caviar from syrup" and much more.
The goal is to draw the attention of the guests to the details, as well as to teach them the culture of drinking.
